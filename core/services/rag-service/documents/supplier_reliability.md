# Supplier Reliability Standards & Performance Metrics

## Supplier Performance Scoring

### Overall Reliability Score Calculation
The Supplier Reliability Score is calculated monthly using weighted metrics:

- **On-Time Delivery (40%)**: Percentage of orders delivered within promised window
- **Quality Acceptance (30%)**: Percentage of shipments accepted without quality issues
- **Documentation Accuracy (15%)**: Correct and complete paperwork with shipment
- **Temperature Compliance (15%)**: Cold chain integrity maintained

### Performance Tiers
- **Platinum (95-100%)**: Preferred supplier, priority allocation, quarterly reviews
- **Gold (90-94%)**: Approved supplier, standard terms, quarterly reviews
- **Silver (85-89%)**: Approved with monitoring, monthly reviews, improvement plan required
- **Bronze (80-84%)**: Probationary status, weekly reviews, 90-day improvement plan
- **Below 80%**: Suspended, cannot accept new orders, re-qualification required

## Weather Risk Assessment

### High Weather Risk Regions
Suppliers in these regions may experience delivery delays during certain seasons:

- **Pacific Northwest** (Oct-Mar): Heavy rain, flooding risks
- **Alaska** (Nov-Apr): Snow, ice, shipping delays
- **Midwest** (Dec-Feb): Blizzards, road closures
- **Southeast** (Jun-Nov): Hurricane season
- **California** (Year-round): Wildfire season (Jul-Oct), potential road closures

### Weather Contingency Planning
- Monitor 7-day weather forecasts for supplier regions
- Pre-order from weather-risk regions when storms predicted
- Activate backup suppliers 48 hours before major weather events
- Increase safety stock for high-risk periods

## Delivery Performance Standards

### On-Time Delivery Targets
- **Standard Delivery**: Within promised 4-hour window
- **Expedited Delivery**: Within promised 2-hour window
- **Emergency Delivery**: Within 12 hours of request

### Acceptable Lead Times by Category
- **Fresh Produce**: 24-48 hours
- **Seafood**: 24-36 hours
- **Dairy**: 48-72 hours
- **Frozen Goods**: 3-5 days
- **Dry Goods**: 5-7 days

## Risk Mitigation Strategies

### Geographic Diversification
- Maintain suppliers in at least 2 different regions per category
- Prefer regional suppliers for time-sensitive products
- Balance between local (speed) and distant (cost) suppliers

### Capacity Buffers
- Critical suppliers must maintain 20% excess capacity
- Backup suppliers tested monthly with small orders
- Emergency supplier list maintained and updated quarterly

### Financial Stability Monitoring
- Annual financial review for critical suppliers
- D&B credit monitoring for suppliers over $500K annual volume
- Payment terms adjusted based on financial health

### Quality Issue Response
- **Minor issues**: Communicate to supplier, monitor next 3 deliveries
- **Major issues**: Immediate corrective action required, hold payment
- **Critical issues**: Stop shipments, switch to backup, formal investigation

## Supplier Development Program

### New Supplier Onboarding
1. Initial assessment and documentation review (Week 1)
2. Facility audit or third-party audit review (Week 2-3)
3. Sample order and quality testing (Week 4)
4. Trial period: 3 months with enhanced monitoring
5. Full approval after successful trial period

### Continuous Improvement
- Quarterly business reviews with top 20 suppliers
- Annual strategic planning sessions
- Shared KPI dashboards
- Collaborative problem-solving workshops
- Technology integration projects (EDI, forecasting systems)

### Supplier Innovation Incentives
- Volume discounts for reliability improvements
- Long-term contracts for platinum tier suppliers
- Co-investment in capacity or technology upgrades
- Preferred supplier status for new product categories

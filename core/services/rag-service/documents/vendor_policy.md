# Vendor Management Policy - Food & Beverage Supply Chain

## Approved Vendor Categories

### Fresh Produce Suppliers
- **Organic Certification Required**: USDA Organic, EU Organic
- **Cold Chain Requirements**: Maintain 34-38째F throughout transport
- **Quality Standards**: Grade A or higher, less than 2% defects
- **Approved Regions**: California, Oregon, Washington, Florida, Mexico (certified facilities)

### Seafood Suppliers
- **Sustainability Certifications**: MSC, ASC, or equivalent
- **Temperature Control**: -10째F for frozen, 32-38째F for fresh
- **Traceability**: Full chain of custody from catch to delivery
- **Testing**: Weekly microbiological testing required
- **Approved Species**: Wild-caught salmon, farmed shrimp, Atlantic cod, yellowfin tuna

### Dairy Suppliers
- **HACCP Certification**: Required for all suppliers
- **Temperature Requirements**: 36-40째F during storage and transport
- **Shelf Life**: Minimum 7 days remaining at delivery
- **Quality Grades**: Grade A milk and dairy products only
- **Approved Regions**: Wisconsin, California, Vermont, New York

## Delivery Requirements

### Standard Delivery Windows
- **Fresh Produce**: 24-48 hours from harvest
- **Seafood**: 24-36 hours from catch/processing
- **Dairy**: 48-72 hours from processing
- **Frozen Items**: 5-7 days acceptable

## Quality Control

### Receiving Inspection
- Temperature verification at receiving dock
- Visual quality inspection (10% sample)
- Documentation verification (COAs, certifications)

### Supplier Scorecarding
- **On-Time Delivery**: Target 95% or higher
- **Quality Acceptance Rate**: Target 98% or higher
- **Documentation Accuracy**: Target 99% or higher
- **Temperature Compliance**: Target 100%
